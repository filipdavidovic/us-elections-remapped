When studying the distribution of the gender of the voters across states, the results seem to follow a normal distribution. We can however notice that the coastal states usually register a majority of women voters and the same states tend to be won by the democrats. On the other hand, the republicans tend to win the majority of the states were there are more men than women. Geographically, those states are the less populated ones in the north-center of the map.
